History


The "CSE CU Association" is a departmental association that includes all students and faculty members of the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department at the University of Chittagong. This association began its journey in 2010 under the name "CSE CU Association." A preparatory committee was formed on January 5, 2010, to officially establish the association. The structure and constitution of the association were approved by the 9th Academic Committee meeting of the department on May 5, 2010. On that date, the name of the association was officially changed from "CSE CU Association" to "CSE CU Association."
